Clinical Services

Includes patient care -providing clinical services needed by patients for their treatment. Development of healthcare services involves deciding what type of healthcare services (e.g., general practice, surgery, specialist services) to provide and how (inpatient, outpatient). This also involves keeping-up the knowledge and skills of existing healthcare staff.
